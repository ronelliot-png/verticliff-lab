The System

The VertiCliff™
Architecture

A story architecture system designed specifically for vertical episodic content. Not adapted from film school. Built from the ground up for the scroll.

01 — STRUCTURE

25 Engineered Cliff Endings

Map every episode ending with precision. Each cliff creates a specific emotional question that makes the next episode impossible to skip.

02 — ESCALATION

Micro-Stakes Architecture

Pressure builds incrementally across every episode. The audience never feels the escalation — they only feel compelled to continue.

03 — CHARACTER

Power Shift Design

Map power volatility between characters so every episode changes who's winning. Static power kills engagement. Shifting power creates addiction.

04 — PACING

Platform-Native Timing

90 seconds structured to precision. Hook at 0–3s. Escalation through the middle. Reversal at 75s. Cliff at the end. Every second has a job.

05 — SERIES

The structural backbone of every great vertical series — mapped specifically to episodic format across 6, 12, 25, or 60 episodes.
